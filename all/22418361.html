about me


Hello!
welcome to my world of color and creativity. My name is Lina Yassin, and I am a passionate artist who finds my voice through the transformative power of painting. Art has always been an integral part of my life, a way to express the depths of emotion, the beauty of the natural world, and the infinite complexity of the human experience. Through my paintings, I invite viewers to see the world through my eyes—through strokes of color, light, and texture.
